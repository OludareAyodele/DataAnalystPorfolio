About Me

I am a graduate of Computer Science with a solid foundation in analytical thinking and problem-solving. My professional journey has led me from working as a Sales Executive in the power sector to pursuing my passion for data analysis. This unique blend of technical education and industry experience enables me to bring a practical, results-oriented approach to data analytics.

During my tenure in the power sector, I honed my skills in data collection, trend analysis, and customer relationship management. This experience has provided me with valuable insights into the operational and sales processes, allowing me to identify key performance indicators and leverage data to drive business growth.

As a Data Analyst, I am proficient in a range of tools and technologies, including Excel, SQL, and data visualization platforms such as Tableau and Power BI.
